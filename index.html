Why we're here

Our purpose

Shadows isn't about claiming territory in the dark — it's about showing up for each other, every session, on SharkSMP.

Shadows started as a small group of friends on SharkSMP who wanted a base, a crew, and a name worth defending. We've grown into a team with real structure — builders shaping the skyline, PvPers holding the line, and devs keeping our Discord running smoothly behind the scenes.

We're not here to be the scariest name on the server. We're here to build something good, fight when it's worth fighting, and make sure everyone on the team — new or veteran — knows exactly where they fit in.

Get in the world

Server info

Everything you need to connect to SharkSMP and find Shadows in-game.

Java & Bedrock IP

Bedrock players: same address, port 19132

How to connect

  1. Open Minecraft & go to Multiplayer
  2. Click Add Server
  3. Paste sharksmp.net as the address
  4. Save, then join & say hi in chat

Rules & support

SharkSMP keeps full rules pinned in their Discord. The short version: no cheating, no griefing outside PvP-sanctioned raids, and treat people the way you'd want to be treated.
